what is skitsup?

skitsup is a videochatting software made for educational settings. With skitsup, communication is increased between teachers, administrators, students, and parents.

anytime, anywhere!

one-on-one interactions

Sometimes, it's difficult to hear from all students every day in the classroom. Make sure all voices and concerns are voiced with skitsup. Confidential one-on-one meetings are great for following up with students, and they are able to speak at ease.
