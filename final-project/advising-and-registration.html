Advising Procedures


Prior to the semester starting, students are responsible for reaching out to their advisor and requesting advising assistance. Students are assigned advisors during the second week of their first semester and should direct questions to Alexis Shinawongse, Academic Program Specialist.


Use the Bookings app to schedule an appointment with Alexis.

To contact or schedule an appointment with your advisor, use your USF email account. Non-USF email messages may be marked as spam and may delay response time.



Career Pathways


Students may use our career pathway guides to assist with elective selection, in addition to suggestions from their advisor.

  • Academic Librarianship
  • Archives Pathway
  • Health Science Librarianship Pathway
  • Public Librarianship
  • School Libraries Pathway
  • Youth Services

Note: Career pathways are not official concentrations and will not be listed on your transcripts.
They are for advising purposes only.



Using your Program Plan


The Program Plan form should be used to track progress throughout the program. It is the student's responsibility to keep this form updated and it must be submitted when requesting advisor's assistance. DegreeWorks does not record graduate level progress.
