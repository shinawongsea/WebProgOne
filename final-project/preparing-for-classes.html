Navigating Canvas



Announcements


Important announcements regarding registration, professional development opportunities, and program updates are posted in the MLIS Canvas Org. Canvas also offers a mobile app for students.This is useful for checking assignment deadlines and receiving announcements on-the-go.

  • Guide: How to adjust Canvas notification settings


Coursework


All coursework is completed in Canvas. One week before the semester begins, verify that your courses are showing on your Canvas Dashboard.

Deadlines are posted in EST to align with the University of South Florida system.

Student Handbook


Students are required to review the 2021-2022 MLIS Student Handbook.

It is important to read through the entire handbook and gain a complete understanding of policies and expectations.

This handbook is a supplement to the 2021-2022 Graduate Catalog (Office of Graduate Studies).

Tuition Payments


Bills are not sent out by the university. Please check your account summary by term on OASIS before the end of the first week of classes to check the balance on your account. To avoid the late payment fee, tuition must be paid by the 5th day of the term if you do not have a tuition deferment.

To pay your account balance:

  1. Login to MyUSF > OASIS (Student)
  2. Tuition and Fees > View Account Summary and Pay My Bill
  3. Student Bill Payment
